Academic Programs

Discover our comprehensive undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to prepare you for careers in technology.

Undergraduate Programs

  • BSc Software Engineering
    Requirements: Math & Physics at A-Level. Outlines include Data Structures, Agile Methods, and AI programming.
  • BSc Computer Science
    Requirements: Math at A-Level. Covers theoretical foundations, Assembly, OS, and advanced Networking.
  • BSc Information Technology
    Requirements: Any 2 principal passes. Focuses on Enterprise Database Admin, Network routing, and IT infrastructure.
  • BSc Data Science & Analytics
    Requirements: Math & standard passes. Focuses on Machine Learning, Big Data, and Statistical Modeling.
  • BSc Cybersecurity & Forensics
    Requirements: Any 2 principal passes. Covers penetration testing, digital forensics, and network defense.
  • Diploma in Hardware & Electronics
    2-year practical course. Focuses on circuit design, IoT systems, and troubleshooting micro-controllers.
  • BSc Artificial Intelligence (AI)
    Requirements: Math at A-Level. Groundbreaking degree focusing heavily on Neural Networks, NLP, and Automation.
  • BSc Information Systems
    Requirements: Any 2 principal passes. Bridges computing and business strategy. Focuses on ERP delivery and IT management.
  • BSc Cloud Computing
    Requirements: Standard passes. Specializes heavily in AWS/Azure architecture, virtualization, and scalable networks.

Postgraduate Programs

  • MSc Computing & Information Systems
    Designed for practicing IT professionals. Advanced modules in Data Science, Cloud Computing and IT Governance.
  • MSc Software Engineering
    Advanced rigorous research into Software Architecture, Dev-Ops pipelines, and scalable enterprise applications.
  • Postgraduate Diploma in IT
    Fast-track transition for non-IT degree holders. Covers fast-paced Programming, Databases, and Routing.
  • PhD in Computer Science
    High-level research degree. Candidates conduct ground-breaking original research under expert faculty supervision.

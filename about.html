About Our Department

Advancing the frontiers of technology through excellence in teaching, research, and service since the department's inception.

Our Heritage & Purpose

The Department of Computing & Informatics at Bugema University stands as a pillar of technological advancement in the region. We are dedicated to providing a transformative educational experience that goes beyond the classroom.

We recognize that the digital landscape is constantly shifting. Therefore, our curriculum is intentionally dynamic, integrating the latest industry trends with a solid foundation in computational theory.

What We Stand For

Our Vision

To be a premier center of excellence in computing education and research, producing globally competitive professionals.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality training in informatics through hands-on practice, innovation, and Christian values.

Core Values

Innovation, Integrity, Excellence in Service, and Christ-centered professionalism in every line of code.

Message from the HOD

"It is my distinct pleasure to welcome you to the Department of Computing & Informatics. We are exceptionally proud to cultivate a culture of innovation, rigorous technical inquiry, and practical skill-building."

Our commitment is to graduate highly proficient professionals who are equipped to solve complex technological challenges. By upholding Christian values alongside academic excellence, we ensure that you are prepared not just for the dynamic IT industry, but for a lifetime of principled leadership.

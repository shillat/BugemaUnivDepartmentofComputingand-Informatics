Our Department

The Department of Computing and Informatics serves as a hub for innovation, providing students with the critical skills needed to excel in the digital age. Our curriculum covers Software Engineering, Network Administration, Artificial Intelligence, and Cybersecurity.

Beyond academics, the department fosters a community through technical clubs and hands-on laboratory sessions. We believe that technology should be a tool for positive community impact and sustainable development.
